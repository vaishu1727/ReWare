About Us

Our Moto

At ReWare, we believe that a piece of clothing can be more than just fabric—it can be warmth for someone in need, dignity for someone struggling, and a second chance for something beautiful. Our mission is simple: to bridge the gap between those who have surplus clothing and those who need it the most.

Through ReWare, anyone can donate pre-loved clothes, and ashrams, NGOs, and individuals can find and claim them—ensuring that no good piece of clothing goes to waste. By fostering a culture of sustainable giving, we aim to reduce textile waste while making a tangible impact on people’s lives.

Join us in rewriting the story of discarded clothes—one donation at a time. Because every thread matters.
